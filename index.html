About this tool. The Legibility Game is an educational simulation designed for cultural workers, artists, and arts administrators. It models the institutional pressures and decision-making environments that shape careers and organisations in the contemporary arts ecosystem. By working through realistic scenarios, you will surface your own decision-making patterns, understand the trade-offs between institutional compliance and critical practice, and develop a more nuanced awareness of how structural forces operate. This is not a test — it is a training ground for thinking clearly under pressure.

Welcome to the Legibility Assessment. This tool measures your institutional fitness across a series of operational scenarios drawn from standard field conditions.

You will face six scenarios. Each requires a decision. Your responses will be processed by our Institutional Compatibility Engine and scored across four dimensions: Contractual Viability, Stakeholder Alignment, Mission Coherence, and Systemic Compliance.

You will not see your scores during the assessment. The system will produce a full Performance Report upon completion. This is by design. In practice, you also cannot see your scores. You discover them when the grant is declined, when the invitation does not arrive, when you are discussed at a gathering you were not invited to.
